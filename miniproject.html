few facts about the world we live in

earth is the third planet from the sun and is the largest of the terrestial planet. The Earth is the only planet known to harbor life, and its biosphere has significantly altered the atmosphere and other abiotic conditions on the planet. The Earth has a powerful magnetic field, the magnetosphere, which deflects the solar wind's charged particles. The Earth's lithosphere is divided into several rigid tectonic plates that migrate across the surface over periods of many millions of years.
